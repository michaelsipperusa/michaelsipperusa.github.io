--- layout: archive title: "Resources & Materials" permalink: /resourcesmaterials/ author_profile: true --- --- layout: archive title: "Resources & Materials" permalink: /resources/ author_profile: true --- {% include base_path %} Free learning materials from the U.S. Department of State and trusted sources --- ## Introduction Welcome to our comprehensive collection of English learning resources, primarily sourced from [American English](https://americanenglish.state.gov), the official U.S. Department of State platform for English language teaching and learning. These materials include classroom activities, audiobooks, MP3s, videos and pedagogical materials to assist in learning English as a second language. All resources are completely free and have been carefully curated to support learners at every proficiency level. Whether you're just starting your English journey or refining advanced academic skills, you'll find materials designed to help you succeed. The resources are organized by proficiency level following international standards (A1-C2), making it easy to find content appropriate for your current abilities. These materials are provided through the Office of English Language Programs in Washington, D.C., which offers academic expertise and resources worldwide through Regional English Language Officers at American Embassies and Consulates. Faculty members can also use these materials for classroom instruction and homework assignments. --- ## How to Use This Page **Step 1:** Identify your English proficiency level: - **Beginner (A1-A2):** Basic understanding, simple phrases, everyday expressions - **Intermediate (B1-B2):** Can handle most situations, understand main ideas of complex text - **Advanced (C1-C2):** Fluent expression, understand virtually everything, academic proficiency **Step 2:** Browse the resources in your level section below **Step 3:** Click the links to access free materials directly from the source **Step 4:** Download, practice, and track your progress! --- ## üå± Beginner Level (A1-A2) *Perfect for those just starting their English learning journey or building foundational skills.* ### Featured Beginner Resources #### Everyday Conversations: Learning American English üì± **Interactive Audio & Text** Intended for sixth and seventh-grade students, this resource features dialogues covering introductions and small talk, shopping, asking for directions, hobbies, and giving advice.
